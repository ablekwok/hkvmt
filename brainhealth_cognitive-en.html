Any mentally stimulating activities

Engagement in cognitive activities is associated with greater cortical and subcortical brain volume,maintaining normal functioning. Activities that train the following domains:

Calculation and logic

Visuospatial ability

Judgment

Information processing speed

Language ability

Classification of cognitive activities

Enhance knowledge learning

  • Attending talks and lectures
  • Visiting museums or exhibitions
  • Reading, writing

Develop hobbies

  • Join interest classes
  • Calligraphy
  • Painting
  • Playing musical instruments

Games

  • Playing mahjong
  • Playing chess
  • Card games
  • Computer games

Recommended Cognitive Games Apps

FunFunGame : 長者腦力訓練遊戲

 

  • There are a total of 8 games, including Number Sorting, Color Matching, Find the Same, Puzzle Pairing, Matching Unit of Measure, Rhythm Game, Memory Challenge, and Cognitive Test. There is a play limit and each game can only be played 2 times per day.
  • Category: Games
  • System Requirements: Android/iOS
  • Language: Chinese
  • Developer: Hamo Production
  • Shows Ads: No
  • Registration: None
  • Price: Free

腦有記 NeuroGym

 

  • "NeuroGym" is a smartphone game featuring mahjong, chess, and calligraphy with easy gameplay and operation.
  • Category: Games
  • System Requirements: Android/iOS
  • Language: Chinese
  • Developer: MedMind Technology
  • Shows Ads: No
  • Registration: Email/Phone
  • Price: Free

智有腦

  • System Requirements: Android/iOS
  • Language: Chinese
