Cognitive Activities

Any mentally stimulating activities

Engagement in cognitive activities is associated with greater cortical and subcortical brain volume, maintaining normal functioning.

Activities that train the following domains:

Language ability

Calculation and logic

Visuospatial ability

Information processing speed

Classification of cognitive activities

Enhance knowledge learning

  • Attending talks and lectures
  • Visiting museums or exhibitions
  • Reading, writing

Develop hobbies

  • Join interest classes
  • Calligraphy
  • Painting
  • Playing musical instruments

Games

  • Playing mahjong
  • Playing chess
  • Card games
  • Computer games
