Establishing Aims

Provide a simple and accurate cognitive screening service for people aged 60 or above in Hong Kong.

Assist organizations in identifying older adults with 'Mild Cognitive Impairment (MCI)' to facilitate early prevention.

Raise awareness of brain health management among the general public.

Advance local research on the preclinical dementia.
