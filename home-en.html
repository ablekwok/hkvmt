Test Introduction

是一項用以預測往後出現認知衰退機率的風險數據模型。CARS能提供個人化的生活風險分析，有助改善生活風險因素，以減低患病風險。

 

The study has been published in 《BMC Geriatrics》。

 

No need to download any Apps.

Start right now with Smartphone / Tablet / Computer

*Supports Chrome and Safari browsers

Cognitive Ageing Risk Score (CARS)

A simple and accurate online cognitive test that has been validated based on individuals aged 60 or above. It assessed episodic memory, attention, information processing speed and visuospatial ability.  The study has been published in 《Aging and mental health》 .

 

No need to download any Apps.

Establishing Aims

Provide a simple and accurate cognitive screening service for people aged 60 or above in Hong Kong.

Assist organizations in identifying older adults with 'Mild Cognitive Impairment (MCI)' to facilitate early prevention.

Raise awareness of brain health management among the general public.

Advance local research on the preclinical dementia.
